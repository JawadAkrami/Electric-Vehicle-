# Electric-Vehicle-

**Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**
-----

This project explores a dataset of electric vehicle (EV) registrations to better understand market trends, adoption patterns, and key characteristics of EVs. The analysis focuses on factors such as vehicle type (BEV vs PHEV), manufacturer, model, electric range, and geographic distribution. Through this exploration, it becomes clear that Battery Electric Vehicles dominate the market, with Tesla leading in registrations, and that EV adoption has grown significantly over time—especially in recent years. The analysis also highlights how EV registrations are concentrated in specific counties and cities, indicating regional differences in adoption. While the dataset provides valuable insights, it includes limitations such as a large portion of vehicles with unknown CAFV eligibility, which affects deeper interpretation. Overall, this EDA provides a structured and insightful overview of the EV landscape.

**Interactive Dashboard (Power BI)**
------

The dashboard builds on the EDA by presenting key insights in a more visual and interactive way. It highlights major trends such as the rapid rise in EV adoption over time, the most popular vehicle models, and the leading cities and counties in EV registrations. It also compares total electric range across models, offering a perspective on both vehicle popularity and performance. Designed for quick exploration, the dashboard allows users to easily understand how EV adoption varies across regions and manufacturers. Together, the visuals provide a clear and engaging summary of the evolving electric vehicle market.
