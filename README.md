
**Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**

------

This project performs an exploratory data analysis of electric vehicle (EV) registrations to uncover key trends in adoption, market composition, and vehicle characteristics. The analysis examines factors such as vehicle type (BEV vs PHEV), manufacturer dominance, model popularity, electric range, and geographic distribution.

Key findings reveal that Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) dominate the market, with Tesla leading registrations through high-demand models such as the Model Y and Model 3. EV adoption has accelerated significantly in recent years, particularly after 2020, reflecting growing consumer demand and technological advancements. Additionally, registrations are highly concentrated in specific counties and cities, highlighting the influence of regional infrastructure and policy support.

The dataset also presents limitations, including a large proportion of vehicles with unknown CAFV eligibility, which constrains deeper policy-related analysis. Despite this, the EDA provides a structured and insightful overview of the evolving EV landscape.



**Interactive Dashboard (Power BI)**

------
The Power BI dashboard translates the EDA findings into an interactive and visually engaging format, enabling users to quickly explore key trends and patterns in EV adoption.

The dashboard highlights:

* The rapid growth of EV registrations over time
* Leading vehicle models and manufacturers
* Geographic concentration across cities and counties
* Comparative analysis of total electric range by model

Designed for intuitive exploration, the dashboard allows users to easily identify how EV adoption varies across regions and manufacturers. Together, the visuals provide a clear, data-driven narrative of the transition toward electric mobility.
